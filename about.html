About Us

Car Rental Company (CRC) was founded in 2002 by visionary entrepreneur Mr. Amjad Karim. Dissatisfied with the outdated, low-quality vehicles offered by traditional rental agencies, he set out to redefine the standard of car rental — blending luxury, innovation, and sustainability.

Today, CRC is a trusted partner for discerning travelers, executives, and automotive enthusiasts seeking premium mobility experiences across the region.

Aligned with the philosophies of our partner brands — from Mercedes-Benz's "The best or nothing" to Audi's vision of a digital, sustainable, and electric future — we curate only the latest, most advanced vehicles for our fleet.
